What is the role of information technology in business?

Information technology or the IT department is a crucial part of any company of business as they monitor and manage almost everything that is to do with information technology and communication systems.

IT in business is ultimately to help the business be more efficient and productive. It has a number of different roles including but not limited to:
- Helping the company be more productive, time = money.
- Optimising business performance
- Safeguarding data and troubleshooting
- Saving the business money
- Improving customer experience, satisfaction and communication
